Law of Reversed Effort

Take a piano teacher, for example. He always says, relax, relax. But how can you relax while your fingers are rushing over the keys? Yet they have to relax. -Aldous Huxley, The Divine Within: Selected Writings on Enlightenment.

Diderot Effect

The Diderot effect is a phenomenon that occurs when acquiring a new possession leads to a spiral of consumption that results in the acquisition of even more possessions.
